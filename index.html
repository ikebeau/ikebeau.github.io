About Me

I graduated in December 2024 with a Bachelor's degree in Data Science from BYU-Idaho, where I developed a strong foundation in data analysis, machine learning, and statistical modeling.

During my first semester at BYU-I, I found I had a key interest in providing & building the tools to then find & make data driven decisions. This interest is what helped me make the jump into the field of data science.
